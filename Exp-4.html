Welcome to Kottayam

Kottayam is a beautiful district in Kerala, India, known for its lush greenery and is one of the oldest human habitats of the Kerala.

About Kottayam

Kottayam is one of the most prosperous towns of Kerala is located at its central part, in the midland of hills and plains and surrounded by Meenachil river on the north, the Western Ghats on the East, the Pampa River on South and the Vembanad lagoon on the West.

Tourist Attractions

  • Illikkal Kallu
  • Ilaveezhapoonchira
  • Mango Meadows Agricultural Theme Park
  • KOTTATHAVALAM WATERFALLS VIEW POINT
  • St. Alphonsa's Tomb

Culture and Heritage

Kottayam is rich in culture and heritage, renowned as the "Land of Letters" due to its contributions to Malayalam literature and printing. It boasts a vibrant mix of religious diversity, natural beauty, and historical landmarks, offering a unique blend of tradition and modernity.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kottayam, Kerala, is during the winter months, from September to February. This period offers pleasant temperatures, making it ideal for sightseeing and enjoying the city's attractions.
